MUSIC

Imagine headphones on, vibing to hidden DJs, tucked into Tirana's Botanical Garden, city skyline dim in the background. Plug in, tune out, lose yourself: headphones on, vibes only, natural sounds blending with beats — intimate, artsy, secret, and totally IG-worthy. It's not a party, it's a silent adventure for the crew who knows.

ART

Step into Tulla Culture Center and make art together like never before — a blank wall, a shared vision, and your creativity in every stroke. Each participant adds a piece, turning individual ideas into a massive, collaborative artwork that lives in the space long after the event. Underground, hands-on, and full of energy, it's a playground for art lovers who want to leave their mark on Tirana.

SPORT

Swap the studio for open air and let the Tirana Zoo become your yoga sanctuary. Flow through each pose surrounded by greenery, open skies, and the subtle hum of nature. It's a practice that feels alive, calm, and inspiring — a serene, unconventional space where yoga meets the peaceful rhythm of the wild.

CULINARY

Roll up your sleeves at Luga e Argjendte and make pasta from scratch — knead, twist, and shape your own fresh noodles while soaking in the scenic vibes. It's hands-on, chill, and full of foodie energy — the perfect mix of craft, creativity, and taste.

HIDDEN GEMS

Tirana Treasure Hunt drops you in Lake Park with a map in hand and a mission: chase hidden clues, uncover your mosaic piece, and vibe through the city's green heart. By the end, everyone links up to assemble a giant, one-of-a-kind artwork — a collaborative, underground masterpiece that's part adventure, part art, all energy.
